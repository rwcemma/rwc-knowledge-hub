★ T1💊 Supplement Ordering
Master Inventory Ordering Workflow
Start here before every ordering session — pull low stock report, work through vendors, close out POs
Doc ↗ Video ↗
+
📊Visual Aid — Full Ordering Workflow▼ expand
1
Open Shopify → duplicate the Stocky tabRight-click → Duplicate so you can reference it while ordering without losing your place.
2
Set report: Individual Vendors · Sort by Depletion Ascending · Show AllLeave all other settings as-is. Review Available column and Depletion dates.
3
Apply the 60-day ruleOrder anything depleting within 60 days. Keep a minimum of 5 units on hand at all times.
4
Create a PO per vendor — switch suggestion to 60 daysConfirm pricing before saving the PO. Check for in-transit orders to avoid over-ordering.
5
Work through vendors one at a time using their individual SOP docsFor each vendor: Log in → Add items → Verify quantities → Check free shipping → Confirm 925 Charlotte → Place order → Record confirmation number.
6
Round up to nearest box/case sizeBoxes of 12, 24, 30, 36. Keeps inventory counts clean.
✓
After each order: Record confirmation # → Mark PO as Fully Paid → Mark as OrderedDo not skip these steps — they maintain inventory accuracy across the system.
⚠️ Do NOT order fromLab companies · Discontinued brands · Bundles · Testing kit suppliers — skip these if they appear in the low stock report.

Shipping Address (all vendors)

925 Charlotte — use for every vendor order.

Quantity Rounding Rule

Round up to the nearest countable box or case size whenever possible: boxes of 12, 24, 30, or 36. Keeps inventory counts clean.

Order Closeout Checklist

  • Confirmation number recorded in Stocky PO
  • PO marked as Fully Paid
  • PO marked as Ordered
  • In-transit orders checked before reordering
